🌧 Rainy Day

Contingency — Zoo or Xpark

CKS Memorial Hall → Taipei 101 → Sun Yat-sen stays the same regardless of weather. Only the afternoon changes.
  • Morning–Early PM
    CKS → Taipei 101 → Sun Yat-sen
    Same as the primary plan — these are all manageable in light rain.
  • Afternoon (light rain)
    Taipei Zoo instead of Maokong
    📍 Brown Line → Taipei Zoo terminusNT$60/pax (≈₱115)
    Same Brown Line from Da'an — Taipei Zoo is the terminus, Maokong Gondola boards from the same station. Just swap one for the other. Zoo has giant pandas, elephants, hippos. Partially covered sections for rainy browsing.
  • Afternoon (heavy rain)
    Skip Zoo — Airport MRT to Xpark + Gloria Outlets
    📍 Taipei Main → Airport MRT → A18 Taoyuan HSRNT$550–600/pax (≈₱1,050–1,150)
    Fully indoor — perfect for a downpour. Airport MRT from Taipei Main to A18 takes ~35 min. Xpark has penguins, jellyfish room, large shark tank; Gloria Outlets is right next door. Budget 2–3 hrs total.
  • Evening
    Shilin Night Market — dinner & shopping
    📍 Jiantan MRT, Exit 1NT$250–380/pax (≈₱480–725)
    Return to Taipei from Xpark via Airport MRT. Note: if doing both Zoo and Xpark, transit between them takes ~75 min — a long day, so pick one or the other.

    📍 Near Sizihwan, Gushan DistrictNT$99/pax (≈₱190)
    A preserved colonial-era consulate on a hilltop overlooking the harbor — less crowded than the big-name stops, with a small café on site.
  • Afternoon
    Pier-2 Art Center, then ferry to Cijin Island
    📍 Yancheng District → Gushan Ferry Pier → CijinNT$30/pax ferry (≈₱60) + NT$320–475/pax bike & extras (≈₱610–910)
    Converted harbor warehouses turned art district, then a 10-min ferry to Cijin's black-sand beach, Rainbow Church, and bike trails — stop by Cijin Tianhou Temple (Kaohsiung's oldest, 1673) right by the ferry dock first.
  • Late Afternoon
    Cijin Lighthouse + Cihou Fort, seafood snack, ferry back, quick Lotus Pond + Confucius Temple stop
    📍 Cijin → Gushan → Lotus Pond, Zuoying DistrictFree (fort/temple) + seafood: your call
    Most visitors skip the uphill lighthouse/fort walk — quieter views over the harbor. Lotus Pond is a fast 30–45 min stop on the way back, just for the Dragon & Tiger Pagodas and Confucius Temple.
  • Evening
    Dinner at Ruifeng Night Market
    📍 Ruifeng Night MarketNT$250–320/pax (≈₱480–610)
    Less touristy than Liuhe, with great seafood snacks.
