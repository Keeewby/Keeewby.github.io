Day 1
01
WED · NOV 18
Cebu → Taipei
CEB → TPE · Arrival day
  • Early AM
    Depart Mactan–Cebu for Taipei Taoyuan
    ✈ CEB → TPE~₱10,900–15,700 r/t (≈NT$5,700–8,200)
    Compare Cebu Pacific, STARLUX, and Philippine Airlines — STARLUX flies the most frequent nonstop.
  • Midday
    Land at Taoyuan, clear immigration, get EasyCards + eSIM
    📍 Taoyuan Airport arrivals hall₱570–1,150/pax eSIM (≈NT$300–600)
    Buy 4 EasyCards at the airport convenience store or MRT counter. For data, buy an eSIM online ahead of time (Klook/KKday/Airalo) so it's active the moment you land, or get one on arrival at the Chunghwa Telecom/Taiwan Mobile/Far EasTone counters right in the arrivals hall.
  • Afternoon
    Quick hop to Xpark Aquarium + Gloria Outlets
    📍 Taoyuan HSR Station (A18), Exit 2NT$30/pax hop (≈₱60) + NT$550–600/pax aquarium (≈₱1,050–1,150)
    Store luggage in lockers at the station first, then enjoy Xpark — inside Landmark Plaza, an 8–10 min walk through Gloria Outlets, budget 2–2.5 hrs, book online via Klook.
  • Late Afternoon
    Retrieve luggage, continue into Taipei, check in at Taipei Sunny Hostel
    📍 Taoyuan HSR Station → Taipei Main StationNT$160/pax (≈₱300)⏱ 45–70 min
  • Evening
    Browse Taipei City Mall, easy dinner, short Ximending stroll
    📍 Ximending / Taipei Main Station areaNT$250–320/pax (≈₱480–610)
    Taipei City Mall is the underground shopping street built right into the station — duck in before you even surface, it's free to browse and a fraction of Ximending's prices. Carrefour Guilin Store (~10–15 min walk) is also a solid one-stop pasalubong run if the mall doesn't have what you need. Keep it light overall — you'll have had a fuller arrival day than usual with the Xpark stop.
Day 2
02
THU · NOV 19
Taipei sights + shopping
Taipei full day
  • Morning
    Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall & Liberty Square
    📍 Zhongzheng DistrictFree
    An easy, gorgeous walk before the day heats up.
  • Midday
    Taipei 101 Observatory (89F) + lunch in Xinyi
    📍 Taipei 101 / XinyiNT$630–790/pax (≈₱1,200–1,500)
    Book a Klook combo ticket ahead of time for a discount over gate price. Free add-on: the Xiangshan Trail starts at this same MRT station and climbs to a classic Taipei 101 skyline view in 20–30 min.
  • Early Afternoon
    Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall
    📍 Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall MRT (Red Line)Free
    One stop from Taipei 101 on the Red Line — grand hall, gardens, and a changing-of-the-guard ceremony on the hour; an easy, low-effort add-on between Taipei 101 and Maokong.
  • Afternoon
    Maokong Gondola — tea-plantation skyline views
    📍 Taipei Zoo Station → MaokongNT$300/pax (≈₱575)
    Crystal cabin (glass floor) is +NT$50/trip (≈₱95) if anyone's feeling brave. Prefer hot springs to a gondola? Beitou's Thermal Valley (free, same Red Line) is a striking alternative.
  • Evening
    Shilin Night Market — dinner & shopping
    📍 Jiantan MRT, Exit 1NT$250–380/pax (≈₱480–725)
    Try the oyster omelet, large fried chicken fillet, and flame-grilled cube steak. Ningxia Night Market is a smaller, more local alternative if Shilin feels too crowded.

Day 4 — Concert Day

SAT · NOV 21 · KAOHSIUNG
Plan A

Concert-goers

  • Morning
    Check out of King's Town Hotel, light breakfast, store luggage
    No second Kaohsiung night is booked — stash bags at the hotel's bag storage (ask at checkout) or in lockers at Zuoying HSR Station before heading out.
  • 10–14:00
    Cijin Island half-day
    NT$320–475/pax (≈₱610–910)
    Ferry from Gushan Pier, bike rental, black-sand beach, Rainbow Church, fresh seafood lunch.
  • 14:30
    Rest, freshen up, get concert-ready
    No hotel room this time around — a café or the station's seating areas work fine for a break before the show.
  • 17:00
    Arrive early — merch & fan zone
    📍 World Games MRT (R17), Exit 1
    ~3–4 min MRT from Zuoying HSR, then 5–8 min walk to the stadium.
  • 19:00
    BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG'
    📍 Kaohsiung National StadiumNT$2,980–9,380/ticket (≈₱5,700–17,950)
    Real-name ticketing via tixcraft — bring ID matching the ticket exactly.
  • Late
    Post-concert snack, head to Zuoying
    NT$190–250/pax (≈₱360–480)
    MRT queues spike right after — grab food, let the crowd thin out, then head toward Zuoying HSR Station.
  • Overnight
    Wait out the night near Zuoying HSR Station
    A long stretch until the first train — look for a 24-hour café or fast-food spot nearby to sit, charge phones, and recharge.
  • ~5:50am (Nov 22)
    First THSR train back to Taipei
    🚄 Zuoying → TaipeiNT$1,490/pax (≈₱2,850)
    Book this leg as its own ticket — early-bird discounts may not apply this close to travel.

Eat & explore

Food & tourist spot picks

Taipei — night markets

  • ₱
    Shilin Night Market
    Oyster omelet, large fried chicken fillet, cube steak — Jiantan MRT Exit 1. NT$50–150/item (≈₱95–285).
  • ₱
    Raohe Street Night Market
    Michelin-listed black pepper buns, herbal pork rib soup — Songshan MRT Exit 5.
  • ₱
    Ningxia Night Market
    Fried taro balls, Cantonese-style porridge, old-school arcade games.

Taipei — spots & shopping

  • ₱₱
    Taipei 101 Observatory
    89F views, world's fastest elevator. NT$630–790/pax (≈₱1,200–1,500).
  • ₱
    Maokong Gondola
    Tea-plantation views, panoramic skyline. NT$300 (≈₱575), unlimited rides.
  • Free
    Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall
    Grand hall, gardens, changing-of-the-guard ceremony on the hour — one stop from Taipei 101.
  • ₱–₱₱
    Dihua Street
    Tea, dried goods, traditional souvenirs — great for mom's list.
  • ₱–₱₱
    Carrefour Guilin Store
    One-stop pasalubong run — local snacks, gifts, halal options. ~10–15 min walk from Ximending.

Kaohsiung — night markets

  • ₱
    Ruifeng Night Market
    Authentic local street food and seafood, near Zuoying.
  • ₱
    Liuhe Night Market
    More souvenir-shopping focused, near Kaohsiung Main Station.
  • ₱₱
    Cijin seafood stalls
    Grilled squid, oysters, crab — straight off the boats.

Kaohsiung — spots

  • Free
    Lotus Pond
    Dragon & Tiger Pagodas, Spring and Autumn Pavilions.
  • Free
    Fo Guang Shan
    Massive Buddhist monastery and museum complex.
  • Free
    Dome of Light
    Glass-art ceiling at Formosa Boulevard MRT — a quick stop for everyone.
  • ₱–₱₱
    Carrefour Market Zuoying
    Souvenir/snack run near your hotel base, open 24h (Chongde branch).
